🍽️ How Are Nutrients Absorbed?

Digestion breaks food into nutrients your cells can absorb and use:

🌾 Carbohydrates
→ Simple sugars (glucose). Brain and muscle energy.
🥩 Proteins
→ Amino acids. Build muscles and enzymes.
🧈 Fats
→ Fatty acids. Long-term energy and organ protection.
💧 Water & Vitamins
→ Absorbed directly. Essential for all body processes.
